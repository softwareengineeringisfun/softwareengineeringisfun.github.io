Sune Chung Jepsen is working as a research assistant at the SDU Software Engineering section. His research interest lies within the areas 1) software architectures for specific types of systems, 2) software architectures for emerging technologies, and 3) organization processes and software architecture strategies.

Sune Chung Jepsen completed his PhD 2022 at the SDU Software Engineering section. His doctoral project aims to investigate software architectures for production systems in relation to Industry 4.0. The research includes architecture evaluation, functional and non-functional requirements for infrastructure platforms that support smart manufacturing systems, as well as implementing and evaluating actual middleware for the systems. Software architecture and non-functional requirements are the main focus in order to facilitate the integration of smart production in the I4.0 Laboratory and companies.
